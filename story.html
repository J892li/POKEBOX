Story

Fresh since 2016


10:00AM,Fall,2016

The Poke Box was founded from a genuine urge to share a meal that is guilt free and exciting to eat. We believe you should always look forward to a meal and feel great about what you eat.The Poke Box began serving delicious poke to Toronto's financial district in Fall 2016.


Where does it come from?

Since then we have established our poke as more than a trend, for our customers it has become a lifestyle food.We are committed to using ingredients that are superior in freshness, flavour and above all, nutrition. We pick and choose our ingredients carefully, working closely with our purveyors on a daily basis to procure only the best.


What products do we offer?

The Poke Box prepares our ingredients on a daily basis, crafts our marinades in small batches and makes each meal to order. The result is an exceptional box, filled with delicious proteins, refreshing vegetables, complex sauces and unique toppings.Indulge in one of our boxes today and say hello to your new routine!
